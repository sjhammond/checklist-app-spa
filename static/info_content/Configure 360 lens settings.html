15F. Configure 360° lens settings

In you are using a circular fisheye lens, or an ImmerVision Enables® Panomorph 360° lens, you must configure it on the Devices > Cameras > Fisheye Lens tab for the Smart Client to correctly dewarp the image.

If you are using a different 360° lens, your camera manufacturer may have a Smart Client plug-in available you can install on the client workstation to enable Smart Client to dewarp that image correctly.
