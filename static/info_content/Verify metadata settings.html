16A. Verify metadata settings

Verify the metadata settings for each metadata source or group of metadata sources on the Devices > Metadata > Settings tab and change them as needed.

Selecting a group allows you to change settings on all the cameras in the group and any subgroups all at once. Because most settings are hardware device driver specific, you can filter the group by hardware device in the dropdown list at the top of the Properties window.
